All My Children


BRIANA

Each One, Teach One

Briana is the oldest as well as the only girl. She is the person I know
that as a child she did not like chocolate candy or ice cream. So strange.
As far back as kindergarten, she has always wanted to be a teacher. She would
take workbooks from her class and bring them home and pretend to be a teacher. Her
two younger siblings would be her students. She gets the credit for my oldest son
Joel's precociousness when he was growing up. She is now working on an Associate's
degree in Early Childhood Education.



JOEL

Automobile Enthusiast

Joel is a automobile aficianado! When he talks about cars his
face lights up like a child on Christmas morning. I am still amazed
at how knowlegable he is about automobile, but I'm not surprised. When Joel was
a little boy he had just about every Hot Wheels piece there was. Each time he
recieved a new one he made sure to flip it over to read the description that was
engraved on the bottom of the toy car. For him, it was not an option. It was
protocol. He is now twenty on years old and he still doesn't break protocol.
Now, however, he makes sure to go online and get all the info on an automobile.
