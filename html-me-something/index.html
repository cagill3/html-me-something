All My Children


BRIANA

Each One, Teach One

Briana is the oldest as well as the only girl. She is the person I know
that as a child she did not like chocolate candy or ice cream. So strange.
As far back as kindergarten, she has always wanted to be a teacher. She would
take workbooks from her class and bring them home and pretend to be a teacher. Her
two younger siblings would be her students. She gets the credit for my oldest son
Joel's precociousness when he was growing up. She is now working on an Associate's
degree in Early Childhood Education.
